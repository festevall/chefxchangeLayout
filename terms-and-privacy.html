SEARCH 2

LOOK FOR THE PERFECT CULINARY EXPERIENCE

Through our search functionality, you can look for a Chef by location, type of cuisine, and date. A list of Chefs will appear based on the criteria of your choice, and you will be able to seamlessly browse through their profiles, the menus they can offer, pictures of the dishes, the Chefs' story, the price they charge for each menu, as well as reading reviews left by other Foodies!

MESSAGE

SEND REQUESTS TO CHEFS OF YOUR CHOICE

After identifying the Chef that will deliver the culinary experience you are looking for, please reach out to him/her through the messaging platform to express your interest and discuss the menu you would like to go for. Chefs will gladly tailor the menu they showcased to your liking and to accommodate any food allergies your or your guests may have. After agreeing on menu and pricing, the Chef will send you a booking request, including all the discussed details, for you to accept. Credit Card payment will then be done through the platform, and all that will be left for you to do is...

ENJOY

YOUR HASSLE FREE CULINARY EXPERIENCE

The Chef will show up at your doorstep at the agreed time, with all the ingredients and crockery necessary (if not available in your kitchen) for the preparation of the meal. You can either sit down and relax with your guests while the Chef prepares; or make it an educational cooking session by watching him work his magic in your kitchen. You will then be served, after which the Chef will clean up the work-space he used and leave you and your guests full and happy. The next day, you will be able to leave a review and let all other wanna be Hosts know how awesome your Chef was!
